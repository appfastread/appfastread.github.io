Frequently asked questions

The essentials, answered.

How does fastread work?

It shows words one at a time at your eye's focal point (RSVP) with ORP highlighting to boost reading speed. Start with a WPM test, then improve with pacing and exercises.

Does it work offline?

Most exercises work offline; your progress syncs to your account when you're back online.

Can I add my own books?

Yes. Add PDF/EPUB files or scan pages with your camera (OCR) to turn them into text.

Is it free?

Core features are free; advanced exercises and unlimited content are offered via a premium subscription.

Which devices are supported?

iPhone and iPad (iOS). Your progress syncs across devices via your account.
